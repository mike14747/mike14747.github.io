About Me

Mike Gullo has worked in the tech field for 30+ years as an electronics technician. His most recent role was in designing, building, programming (including writing custom bios modifications for graphics cards and configuration files for running them) and maintaining a sizable array of nearly 300 high-end computer servers.

He's currently attending Case Western Reserve University transitioning into his new career as a programmer. He already has many years of experience in PHP, MySQL, HTML and CSS. This latest schooling (at CWRU) will enrich and expand his expertise into the other areas necessary to be a Full-Stack developer.

Often found in the kitchen making healthy, nutritious and (most importantly) great tasting meals. This is more than just a mild hobby.

He serves on the planning committee and as a volunteer for the National Health Association (a 70+year old organization focused on health and wellness).

When he isn't at his computer or in the kitchen, he loves cycling, gardening, hiking the national parks, attending health/nutrition conferences. His most recent trip to Peru was one of his most memorable.

Baseball (as a fan and spectator) is another passion. His interest in statistics and data analysis makes a perfect match with the game that is so heavily statistic oriented.
